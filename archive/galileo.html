Galileo Orbiter Data Archive

Main target body: Jupiter
Other target bodies: Dust, and asteroids Ida, Gaspra, and Dactyl
Launch date: 18 October 1989
951 Gaspra encounter: 29 October 1991
243 Ida and Dactyl encounter: 28 August 1993
Jupiter orbit insertion: 8 December 1995
End of mission: 21 September 2003

Small Bodies Node Asteroid and Dust Subnode curates data from the Galileo Dust Detector Subsystem (DDS), and derived data on the asteroids Gaspra, Ida, and Dactyl. Other links to Galileo data elsewhere in PDS are provided for convenience.
